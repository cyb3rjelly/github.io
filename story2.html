Part 1: Interstellar Tango

In the vast expanse of space, aboard a sleek red spaceship adorned with cherries, three friends found themselves lost in the rhythm of jazz. Mateo, the seasoned astronaut, was at the controls, navigating through the cosmos with precision. Cornellia, the enigmatic space girl, lounged by the observation deck, her eyes fixed on the twinkling stars. And Adam, the jovial technician from America, was indulging in a spontaneous dance, his moves guided by the infectious melody.
