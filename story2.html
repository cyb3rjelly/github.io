Galactic Odyssey - Part One: Interstellar Tango

Mateo
Cornellia
Adam

When the ship finally came to a halt, they found themselves on an alien world, planet Z-76HF21. Outside, diamond rain shimmered against the red hull, while blue dirt crunched underfoot. Yellow plants swayed eerily in the thick fog that enveloped the landscape.

Curiosity piqued, the trio ventured forth, unaware of the danger lurking ahead. Suddenly, a deafening rumble echoed through the mist, and a colossal ball, weighing a million tons, emerged from the shadows. With unrelenting force, it rolled towards them, crushing everything in its path.

"Run!" Mateo shouted, adrenaline coursing through his veins as they dashed through the alien terrain. Cornellia stumbled, but Adam caught her just in time, pulling her to safety as the behemoth ball thundered past.

Breathless but unharmed, they took refuge behind a rocky outcrop, watching in awe as the ball continued its destructive rampage. Determined to find a way off this perilous planet, they pressed onward, navigating through the labyrinthine landscape.

Their journey led them to an ancient temple, where they discovered a hidden portal pulsating with energy. With no other option in sight, they braved its unknown depths, hoping it would lead them home.
